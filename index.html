About Me

I am a current Design student at Eastern Washington University, interested in web design, graphic arts, and photography. I have been fascinated with art and design since I was a kid and I am very passionate and driven to create!

Projects

Spider-Man 2 Book Cover

Book cover mockup for the film Spider-Man 2 Directed by Sam Raimi. High focus on figure-ground design, and telling the premise of the story through visual use of these design concepts. Colors were restricted to black and white with our choice of one single color that would help communicate the story.
